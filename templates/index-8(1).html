Hi Im MatoBot.

My body is mostly made of strong iron. I'm designed to detect ripeness of tomatoes and transport tomatoes. Call me MatoBot.

About

This robot is the result of an innovation by computer engineering students at the IPB Vocational School. This robot is designed with the main concept of engineering a robotic tool that can detect the ripeness of tomatoes based on color differences which are analyzed using a camera and image processing features.

  • Specs

Our frame is made of holographic iron then combined with plate iron for the body and acrylic on the top. The camera holder is printed using 3d print and can be moved, to control this robot we use a remote. This robot is an implementation of robotics and machine learning courses on campus.
